About Me

I have more than 12 years of programming experience in delivering enterprise software. Working as a Software Architect/Full Stack Developer allows me to create useful solutions and solve challenging issues. I also improved my capacity to share my knowledge by working as a professor of Computer Science for more than 3 years. For the last 5 years I'm part of the software architecture department at the Ceará Development Unit.
